MY HOBBIES
Life without a hobby is certainly boring, so here are my hobbies.



SEPAK TAKRAW
Favorite hobby
Sepak takraw is my favorite sport and hobby. With exciting acrobatic feats, this sport captured my interest when I was 9 years old. Since then, I started playing sepak takraw and became increasingly skilled, so much so that by the age of 11, I began representing my school from primary to secondary level. My main positions are killer and feeder. I could play comfortably in all three positions.
FISHING
Relaxing Hobby
As a weekend hobby, fishing is my relaxing pastime with my father or friends to spend time together. With a peaceful natural atmosphere and aggressive fish bites, this hobby is very satisfying.
