BIODATA


My name is Muhamad Arieq Ayassy bin Ariffin and I am 21 years old. I am the eldest of two siblings. I was born on June 6, 2005, in Kelantan, but I was raised and have lived in Nilai, Negeri Sembilan, since I was a child. My father, Ariffin bin Che Rasid, is 52 years old, and my mother, Arnita binti Anwar, is 53 years old. My younger sister, Nur Arieana Ramadhani binti Ariffin, is 18 years old.My father is a technician, while my mother is a full-time homemaker. My younger sibling just finished school and is enjoying their free time. After completing my diploma, if I'm lucky, I will continue my studies to the next level.

I don't think it is possible to say my family is the most supportive and loving one in the world, but for sure I owe them a lot, because they have been there for me from the day I was born until now when I am already an adult. Right at the beginning of my life, they filled me with their love, care, and protection. They went through a lot of difficulties just to ensure that I would be brought up in a very pleasant and safe environment.
